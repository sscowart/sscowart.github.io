Southside Cowart Place Neighborhood Association

in beautiful downtown Chattanooga, TN

To unite our efforts, to obtain and maintain a safe, clean, caring neighborhood in which all people can safely live and work in harmony.
{% include about.header.html text="Our Members" %}

Southside Cowart Place Neighborhood includes the homes and businesses within 12th Street and 20th Street (north to south), and both sides of Broad and Market (west to east). The neighborhood association is comprised of home owners, residents, renters, and businesses. Members pay an annual fee of $25 each calendar year. While this small fee reduces the association's cost of small initiatives, our real capital is human. As an involved, informed, and organized group of neighbors we can continue to make Cowart Place, Southside, and Chattanooga great.

{% include about.header.html text="Our Structure & Board" %}

Established in 2011, in 2016 we reorganized our Board from a traditional (vertical) structure to a area (matrix) structure. Board members, elected for a calendar year, maintain traditional titles such as President, Vice President, Treasurer, and Secretary, however board members are assigned specific Adminstrative and Functional areas of focus representing the various concerns of our neighborhood.
